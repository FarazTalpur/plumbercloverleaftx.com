Key Benefits of Sewer Drain Cleaning

  • Prevention of Blockages: Regular cleaning prevents the accumulation of debris, grease, and other materials that can cause serious clogs.

Specialized Sub-Services

Hydro Jetting

Hydro jetting uses high-pressure water jets to remove stubborn blockages and build-up from pipes. This method is particularly effective for clearing out tree roots that can infiltrate and damage sewer lines.

Video Camera Inspections

A crucial diagnostic tool, video camera inspections allow plumbers to pinpoint the exact location and cause of a blockage, ensuring targeted and efficient intervention.

Drain Snaking

This method employs a flexible auger to dislodge and clear minor blockages, offering a cost-effective solution for routine maintenance.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common signs include slow draining sinks or tubs, gurgling noises from drains, unpleasant odors, water backups, and frequent toilet clogs.

It is recommended to have your sewer drains professionally cleaned every 18 to 24 months to prevent buildup and blockages.

While minor clogs can often be cleared with a plunger or drain snake, professional cleaning is advised for more persistent or severe blockages to ensure thorough cleaning and to avoid damage to pipes.

Professionals commonly use techniques like hydro jetting, drain snaking, and video camera inspections to clean and diagnose issues in sewer drains.

Chemical drain cleaners can be harsh on pipes and are often not effective for severe clogs; professional methods are usually safer and more effective long-term solutions.
